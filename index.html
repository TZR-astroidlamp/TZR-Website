WELCOME TO TZR!

The ESports team run by kids.




Who are we? Well we are a self-made ESports team that is run by kids.
Every man has a tale, and our story starts with just a cool idea for a clan name.
This idea sparked into a small group, a group turned into a team, and a team into an organization.
We play a wide range of games, and we destroy competition at any age, to prove we are not just a group of kids.
